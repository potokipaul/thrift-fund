Thrift & Fund

Donate for a cause and shop with a purpose

Thrift & Fund is a community funded, fully sustainable thrift store where every donation and purchase goes straight back to making an impact in your community. We believe that buying good means doing good to build a better world. When you donate with a cause, you can shop with a purpose.

Here is how it works

Tri-cities’ newest thrift store! In part with The Low Entropy Foundation, where all proceeds go toward funding free programs for your community.

Donate

Make space in your life by donating gently used clothing and items.

Rejuvenate

Donated items are sorted, spruced up, and given new life. Any item not suitable for sale is fully recycled.

Resell

Rejuvenated items are sold at unbeatable prices both online and in the Thrift & Fund store.

Impact

All proceeds go straight back to Low Entropy’s programs, providing food security, job training, community-building, and so much more!
